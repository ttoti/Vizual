Vizual

Vizual is a music visualizer web application that is powered by the following technologies: Python, Flask, SQLite, Bootstrap, HTML5, and Javascript.

With this application, one can register, submit songs and view a feed of posts; if you're not interested in signing up, you still have access to the visualizer by clicking the player tab.

A project by Elias Gomez, Tomas Hernandez, Dylan Montgomery and Garred Murphy for CST 205 at California State University, Monterey Bay
